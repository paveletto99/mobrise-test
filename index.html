EAT GOOD

Eating Italian means eating well. The main foods such as oil, cereals, vines and vegetables are the basis of the Mediterranean diet that in 2010 became a World Heritage Site. Italian food stands out for the healthiness, quality and authenticity of the raw materials.

ITALIAN WAY

Italian food is a way of life, with conviviality at the center whether it's lunch, dinner or an aperitif. Meals are eaten in company, together with family or friends. Finally, Italian food is creativity and tradition and every dish tells a story.

ENJOY THE PLANET

The Italian products and producers that agrigud selects respect the territory and biodiversity. Many products are still handmade and do not contain chemical additives, so as to enhance their tastes and aromas. Eating Italian also means safeguarding our planet.
Agrigud was born to help small Italian artisans to reach consumers all over the world in the most innovative and sustainable way. The passion for quality food has always been part of our culture and now we want to pass it on to our customers through excellent products, which follow the values of authenticity, sustainability and high quality. Finally we firmly believe that our traditions can give us a green and clean future.
